WELCOME

Hello everyone! In this website I will talk about how my life was during the nearly 5 months I lived abroad thanks to the Erasmus+ program. My city of choice was Wroclaw in Poland and I couldn't have been any happier with the choice. I hope that this will be insightful and helpful if you are unsure whether or not to go on such an adventure!

Here you will find more about what it was like to live in the city of Wroclaw. Which were the best places to go out, what to do, which activities to participate, what to visit, etc. Besides these fun things, I will also talk about studies and stuff like that.
